Overview

Built an Instagram page to showcase some of my hip-hop artwork, and quickly went viral due to several artists with millions of followers (DJ Khaled, Big Sean, Tory Lanez, and more) co-signing my page via posting my content on their own page. The page has grown tremendously which has allowed me to work with several artists, music producers, and labels. I was able to capitalise on my following by launching my own merchandise store in which I sell my designs on various merchandise products. Check out the page @a1editsrap .
